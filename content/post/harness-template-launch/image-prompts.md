## cover.webp — 文章封面

**全局调色板**（与博客 Ghost Protocol 风格一致）：
- 背景: deep navy `#07090f` 或 `#0d1117`
- 主辅: teal `#2dd4bf`（主调）
- 副辅: amber `#f59e0b`
- 第三色: purple `#a78bfa`

**Prompt**:

A cinematic isometric illustration of a clean, modern engineering scaffold rising out of darkness — depicting an open-source project template materializing as architecture. In the foreground, a translucent blueprint grid floats above a glowing teal foundation; on the grid, geometric "modules" assemble themselves into a stack: spec → plan → build → review → ship — represented as five floating glass tiles ascending in a staircase, each tile faintly lit with teal `#2dd4bf` edges and amber `#f59e0b` accent dots. The overall composition is balanced and confident, with a subtle ambient glow suggesting "this is real, not vapor". Background: deep navy `#07090f`. No human figures. No literal text on the tiles. Style keywords: clean tech illustration, dark mode aesthetic, minimal and elegant. 16:9 aspect ratio.

---

## 使用说明

1. 把上面 prompt 输入 AI 图片生成工具（推荐 Midjourney v7、Imagen 4、DALL-E 3、Ideogram 2）
2. 16:9 比例，分辨率 1920x1080 或更高
3. 生成后保存到本目录，PNG 或 WebP 都行（PNG 我会帮你转 WebP）
4. 文件名严格用 `cover.webp`（或 `cover.png`，会被转换）
