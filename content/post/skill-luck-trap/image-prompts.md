# 文章配图生成 Prompt

生成后将图片保存到本目录，格式为 .webp 或 .png（会自动转为 .webp），文件名与文章中引用一致。

## 视觉方案

- Primary skill: `guige-hand-write-pic`
- Primary style/layout/aspect: `morandi-journal` / `grouped-cards + metaphor` / `9:16 portrait`
- Why: 这篇文章是人生随想和认知复盘，不是技术文章；温暖手账式 sketchnote 更能承载“中年之后看选择、努力和运气”的沧桑感。
- Per-image overrides: `none`

统一视觉约束：米色或暖白纸张背景，手写笔记感，轻微手绘抖动线条，Morandi 低饱和配色，短中文标签，旧物、纸张、时间线、岔路、便签、印章、折角、铅笔、胶带等手账元素。不要深色科技背景，不要赛博霓虹，不要写实电影海报，不要公司 Logo，不要赌场感，不要复杂 UI 面板，不要大段文字。

## 1. cover.webp — 文章封面

Skill/style: `guige-hand-write-pic / morandi-journal / 9:16 portrait`
Role: cover
Intent: 传达“人生结果是努力和运气共同翻出的牌”，但气质要像一张温暖、克制的人生手账页。

Create a one-page hand-drawn journal-style visual summary on warm cream paper.

Title at top, handwritten Chinese, exact text:「努力 + 运气」

Main visual: center shows one gently turning paper card, not a casino card. The left half is labeled「努力」with small doodles of footsteps, desk lamp, notebook, keyboard, and stacked work papers. The right half is labeled「运气」with doodles of wind, dice, changing weather, and branching paths. Below the card, a small figure stands quietly with a backpack, looking at the card.

Layout: calm centered metaphor, generous whitespace, 3 small sticky-note labels around the card:
- 当下选择
- 未来发牌
- 长期胜率

Footer takeaway, exact text:「别用一张牌审判整个人生」

Style: warm cream paper background #F5F0E8, hand-drawn sketchnote, black marker outlines, Morandi muted teal, dusty orange, soft lavender, warm gray, coral red highlights. Slight paper texture, taped corners, pencil annotations. No dark tech, no neon, no realistic hand, no casino table, no photorealism.

## 3. career-crossroads.webp — 跳槽以后世界变了

Skill/style: `guige-hand-write-pic / morandi-journal / 9:16 portrait`
Role: section explainer
Intent: 表达“选择发生在当下，结果发生在未来；当下最优不等于未来必胜”。

Create a one-page hand-drawn sketchnote on warm cream paper.

Title at top, handwritten Chinese, exact text:「选择在当下，结果在未来」

Main visual: a person stands at a soft hand-drawn fork in the road. Left path has a small office building with labels「更高薪水」「离家更近」「岗位更好」. Right path has an older office building labeled「原公司」. Above the road, draw floating paper notes representing future variables:
- 行业转冷
- 战略调整
- 新老板
- 新技术

Add a small timeline ribbon from「现在」to「几年后」with question marks and weather doodles, showing that future conditions are hidden.

Footer takeaway, exact text:「当下最优，也要接受未来变量」

Style: warm Morandi journal, cream paper, black hand-drawn outlines, muted teal path, dusty orange warning notes, soft lavender future fog. No corporate logos, no dark tech interface, no neon, no photorealistic city.

## 4. marriage-variables.webp — 婚姻里人也是变量

Skill/style: `guige-hand-write-pic / morandi-journal / 9:16 portrait`
Role: section metaphor
Intent: 表达“择偶不是选择一张静态简历，人会变，关系会变”。

Create a warm hand-drawn journal visual on cream paper.

Title at top, handwritten Chinese, exact text:「人也是变量」

Main visual: two simple human silhouettes standing beside two gently diverging life paths. Do not make it romantic or dramatic. Around them are small profile cards that look like paper notes, labeled:
- 收入
- 外貌
- 家庭
- 温柔

Then show a soft timeline arc labeled「5年」「10年」「20年」. Along the arc, small doodles show people changing: sprout growing, weather changing, heart cooling/warming, shoulders carrying responsibility.

Add a small handwritten note:「所谓命，常常是变量展开后的结果」

Footer takeaway, exact text:「婚姻不是静态简历」

Style: hand-drawn, warm cream paper, Morandi muted colors, gentle sketch lines, paper tape, pencil marks, simple icons. Avoid wedding rings, roses, wedding dresses, melodrama, rating stars, scorecards, dark background, cyber UI.

## 5. outcome-bias.webp — 结果偏见

Skill/style: `guige-hand-write-pic / morandi-journal / 9:16 portrait`
Role: concept explainer
Intent: 解释“结果偏见”：不要用最后输赢倒推当初决策质量。

Create a one-page educational sketchnote on warm cream paper.

Title at top, handwritten Chinese, exact text:「别用结果审判过程」

Main visual: a hand-drawn balance scale. Left side labeled「当初选择」with notebook, question mark, and incomplete information icons. Right side labeled「最后结果」with a big stamp「赢 / 输」that looks visually heavier. Behind the scale, draw 3 faint branching paths labeled「可能性 A」「可能性 B」「可能性 C」to show alternate histories.

Add 3 compact cards:
- 好决策 + 坏运气 ≠ 错
- 坏决策 + 好运气 ≠ 对
- 复盘过程，不只看结果

Footer takeaway, exact text:「结果能说明一些，但不能说明全部」

Style: guige-hand-write-pic morandi-journal, cream paper, black marker outlines, muted teal and dusty orange, coral red warning stamp, simple arrows and dotted lines. No judge, no courtroom, no dark tech, no glossy 3D, no complex UI.

## 使用说明

1. 将上述 prompt 分别输入 AI 图片生成工具
2. 默认使用 9:16 竖图，分辨率至少 1080x1920
3. 生成后保存到本目录（PNG 或 WebP 均可，后续会统一转为 WebP）
4. 文章中已经用 `![描述](文件名.webp)` 格式引用了这些图片
